# /are-you-sure

**Description:** Critically verify that recent changes actually achieved the intended outcome, and identify any mistakes or regressions before moving on.

---

## Prompt

Before finalizing, do a critical verification pass on the most recent changes.

1. Re-check the final steps and outcomes against the original intent.
2. Actively look for mistakes, regressions, edge cases, or incomplete work.
3. Be analytical and skeptical rather than assumptive.
4. If anything is wrong or unclear, call it out explicitly and propose a concrete fix.
5. If everything checks out, briefly confirm why it is correct.

Focus on catching issues early so they can be fixed immediately.
